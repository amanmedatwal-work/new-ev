Frequently Asked Questions

What is the charging speed?

Our ultra-fast chargers operate at 180kW, providing up to 80% charge in roughly 20-30 minutes, depending on your vehicle's capacity.

Are the stations available 24/7?

Yes, all Jakhars Power Point stations are operational 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, with round-the-clock surveillance.

Which connectors do you support?

Our charging guns primarily support the standard CCS2 connector, which is widely compatible with modern EVs in India.
