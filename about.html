Our Mission

At Jakhars Power Point, we are building a seamless, high-speed, and ultra-reliable EV charging network that ensures you spend more time on the road and less time waiting.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for sustainable energy, Jakhars Power Point started with a simple vision: to eliminate range anxiety for EV owners in India.

We realized that the transition to electric mobility required robust, fast, and reliable infrastructure. Since our inception, we have rapidly expanded our network, deploying cutting-edge 180kW DC fast chargers across key highways and urban centers to ensure you never run out of juice.
